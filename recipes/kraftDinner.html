Pasta Recipe

Description

Kraft Original Mac & Cheese Dinner is a convenient boxed dinner. Kids and adults love the rich taste of macaroni with cheesy goodness.

Our handy dinners include macaroni pasta and original flavor cheese sauce mix, so you just need milk and margarine or butter to make a tasty side, snack or addition to a meal.

Ingredients

  • Kraft Macaroni and cheese box
  • 1 cup of cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of milk

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Add the macaroni and set a 8 minute timer
  3. Drain the macaroni
  4. Add the Kraft secret powder
  5. Add 1 cup of milk
  6. Add 1 cup of cheddar cheese
  7. Mix
  8. Eat!
