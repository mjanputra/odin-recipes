Pasta Recipe

food typically made from an unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or other shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking.

Description

Ingredients

  • Any Pasta
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Boil water at high heat, wait till boil
  2. Put the pasta of choice in the boiling water
  3. Add salt to boiling water
  4. Set a timer for 8 minutes, check ifd aldente
  5. Drain the pasta
  6. Set Pasta in pan
  7. Add 1tbsp of butter on top of pasta
  8. Mix
  9. Eat!
