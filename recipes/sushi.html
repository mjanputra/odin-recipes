Sushi Recipe

Now widely recognized as the quintessential Japanese dish, sushi is a bite-sized dish of vinegared rice and sliced fish, often wrapped in a sheet of seaweed. Originally, the fish was stored between layers of rice in jars to preserve the protein.

Description

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Vinegar
  • Avocado
  • Tuna
  • Salmon
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Chop filling into strips or sticks
  3. Mix Sugar and vinegar. Combine it with rice
  4. On sushi bamboo mat, spread out 1 sheet of nori
  5. Cover it thinly while leaving 1" strip of uncovered nori
  6. In the centre of Nori, lay out horizontally lines of your filling
  7. Roll up the sushi
  8. Chop the roll
  9. Eat!
